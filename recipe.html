Teddy Bear Toast

Description

Today we're going to learn how to make Teddy Bear Toast! It's not only a big hit with both kids and adults, but it's also fun and easy to make! Just look at how cute it is...




You can even make it with Nutella!



Ingredients

To make this adorable snack, you only need 2 minutes and the following ingredients...

  • A slice of bread
  • A banana
  • Creamy peanut butter (or Nutella)
  • Raisins
  • A toaster!


Directions

The steps are as easy as 1, 2, 3!

  1. Toast the piece of bread in the toaster.
  2. Spread the creamy peanut butter/ Nutella all over it.
  3. Cut up the bananas into thin slices and put two at the top corners and one in the middle for teddy's ears and mouth.
  4. Add three raisins to make the bearâ€™s eyes and nose.

That's it! You're done! A great way to sneak in some healthy options (bananas and raisins) without kids - or adults - complaining!
